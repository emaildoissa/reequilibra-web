# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Sales professionals and financial consultants managing leads through qualification stages. They work on both desktop (during office hours, focused work) and mobile (on the go, quick updates). The primary context is daily pipeline management — triaging new leads, sending proposals, and tracking closed deals.

## Product Purpose

Reequilibra CRM helps financial sales teams track leads through a structured pipeline (triagem → proposta → faturamento fechado), manage tasks and calendar, and gain visibility into sales performance. Success means reducing lead leakage and accelerating time-to-close.

## Brand Personality

Modern, Trustworthy, Efficient. The brand should feel like a professional tool you trust with your pipeline — not a toy, not a legacy corporate monolith. The dark theme with violet accent signals confidence and a premium feel.

## Anti-references

Traditional corporate CRMs with dense, overwhelming interfaces (legacy Salesforce, Zoho CRM). Overstyled "AI startup" landing pages with gradient text and glassmorphism for decoration. The UI should never feel busy or require a training course to navigate.

## Design Principles

1. **Clarity over density** — each screen focuses on the task at hand. No feature is added without a clear user benefit.
2. **Speed and efficiency** — common actions (viewing a lead, changing pipeline stage, messaging on WhatsApp) are one click away.
3. **Trust through polish** — clean dark UI with consistent spacing, purposeful motion, and professional-grade fit and finish.
4. **Mobile parity** — core workflows work equally well on mobile and desktop. The mobile nav and sidebar are both first-class citizens.
5. **Progressive disclosure** — surface the essential, reveal detail on demand. The dashboard shows the overview; drilling into a lead shows the full picture.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA. Sufficient color contrast throughout (body text ≥4.5:1, large text ≥3:1). Keyboard navigable. Screen reader friendly labels. Reduced motion respected. The dark theme must maintain legibility — light gray text on tinted dark backgrounds must be tested for contrast.
